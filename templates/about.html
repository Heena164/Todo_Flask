About

This is a simple Todo list application built using Flask.

Features:

  • Add new todos with a title and description.
  • View and manage existing todos.
  • Update the title and description of a todo.
  • Delete a todo.

This application is designed to help you stay organized and manage your tasks effectively. You can easily add new todos, update their details, and mark them as completed once you've finished the task. The application provides a user-friendly interface to help you keep track of your todos with ease.

Whether you're planning your day, making a shopping list, or organizing a project, this Todo list application can assist you in staying on top of your tasks.

Thank you for using our Todo list application!
